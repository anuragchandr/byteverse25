Why Nutrition is Important?

Nutrition fuels your body and brain. In a country like India, where malnutrition and obesity coexist, awareness and monitoring are essential to uplift health standards.

  • 35%+ of Indian children under 5 are underweight
  • Over 50% of Indian women suffer from iron-deficiency anaemia
  • Protein energy malnutrition affects both rural and urban populations

About NutriSetu

NutriSetu allows users to add food items to calculate calories, protein, fats, and vitamins. Our intelligent analysis compares your meal against standard dietary needs. If nutrients are deficient, the system suggests appropriate additions like protein-rich foods. Once all nutritional metrics are met, you can:

  • Search for matching healthy recipes
  • Order food from our partnered marketplace
